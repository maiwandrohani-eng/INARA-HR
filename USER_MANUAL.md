## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Overview

The INARA HR Management System (HRIS) is a comprehensive human resources management platform designed to streamline HR operations, employee self-service, and organizational management. This system provides end-to-end HR functionality from recruitment to exit management.

### 1.2 Key Features

- **Employee Self-Service Portal**: Employees can manage their profile, submit requests, and access information
- **Leave & Attendance Management**: Automated leave request and approval workflows
- **Timesheet Tracking**: Project-based time tracking with approval workflows
- **Recruitment Management**: Complete applicant tracking system (ATS)
- **Performance Management**: 360-degree reviews and performance evaluations
- **Payroll Management**: Comprehensive payroll processing with multi-level approvals
- **Learning & Development**: Training courses and certification tracking
- **Benefits & Compensation**: Benefits enrollment and compensation management
- **Exit Management**: Resignation workflows and exit interviews
- **Analytics & Reporting**: HR metrics and custom reports

### 1.3 System Requirements

- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Internet Connection**: Stable internet connection required
- **Credentials**: Valid username and password provided by HR

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 First-Time Login

1. **Navigate to Login Page**
   - Go to the INARA HR login URL
   - You'll see the login page with email and password fields

2. **Enter Your Credentials**
   - Enter your email address (provided by HR)
   - Enter your temporary password (if first-time login)
   - Click "Login"

3. **Email Verification**
   - If your email is not verified, you'll receive a verification email
   - Click the verification link in the email
   - Or use the "Resend Verification Email" option on the login page
   - Once verified, log in again

4. **Change Your Password** (Recommended)
   - After first login, navigate to your profile
   - Click "Change Password"
   - Enter your current password and new password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Click "Update Password"

### 2.2 Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

- **Quick Stats**: Leave balance, pending approvals, upcoming deadlines
- **Quick Links**: Access to frequently used features
- **Recent Activity**: Your recent submissions and approvals
- **Notifications**: Important announcements and reminders

### 2.3 Navigation

The sidebar navigation is organized into logical sections:

- **My Workspace**: Personal dashboard, personnel file, organization chart
- **My Requests**: Leave requests, timesheets, travel requests
- **HR Management**: All HR administration features (HR staff only)

## 3. Employee Self-Service

### 3.1 Personal Profile

#### View Your Profile
1. Click "My Personnel File" in the sidebar or dashboard
2. View your personal information, employment details, and documents
3. Information is organized in tabs:
   - **Personal Info**: Name, contact details, emergency contacts
   - **Employment**: Position, department, contract details
   - **Documents**: Contracts, certificates, and other documents
   - **Resignations**: Track your resignation status (if applicable)

#### Update Personal Information
1. Navigate to "My Personnel File"
2. Click "Edit" on any section
3. Update the information
4. Click "Save"

**Note**: Some fields may require HR approval for changes.

### 3.2 Leave Requests

#### Submit a Leave Request

1. **Navigate to Leave Management**
   - Click "My Requests" → "Leave Request" in the sidebar
   - Or use the quick link on the dashboard

2. **Fill Out Leave Request Form**
   - **Leave Type**: Select from available leave types (Annual, Sick, Personal, etc.)
   - **Start Date**: Select your leave start date
   - **End Date**: Select your leave end date
   - **Reason**: Provide a reason for your leave
   - **Notes**: Add any additional information

3. **Submit Request**
   - Click "Submit Leave Request"
   - Your request will be routed to your supervisor for approval
   - You'll receive email notifications on status updates

#### View Leave Balance

1. Navigate to "Leave Request" page
2. Your leave balance is displayed at the top:
   - Available days per leave type
   - Used days
   - Remaining balance

#### Track Leave Request Status

- **Pending**: Waiting for supervisor approval
- **Approved**: Approved and leave days deducted from balance
- **Rejected**: Request rejected (reason provided)
- **Cancelled**: Request cancelled by you

### 3.3 Timesheet Submission

#### Submit a Timesheet

1. **Navigate to Timesheets**
   - Click "My Requests" → "Timesheets" in the sidebar

2. **Select Reporting Period**
   - Choose the start and end date for your timesheet period
   - Typically weekly or bi-weekly periods

3. **Enter Project Hours**
   - For each day in the period:
     - Select project name
     - Enter hours worked (0-24)
   - You can add multiple projects per day
   - System calculates total hours automatically

4. **Review and Submit**
   - Review your hours for accuracy
   - Total hours per project and overall are displayed
   - Click "Submit Timesheet"
   - Timesheet is routed to your supervisor for approval

#### View Previous Timesheets

1. Navigate to "Timesheets" page
2. View all submitted timesheets in a table:
   - Period dates
   - Total hours
   - Status (Pending, Approved, Rejected)
   - Approval date (if approved)

3. **View Details**
   - Click on a timesheet to view the full grid
   - See hours per project per day
   - Download as PDF if needed

#### Export Timesheet PDF

1. Open a timesheet from the list
2. Click "Export PDF" button
3. PDF includes:
   - Employee information
   - Reporting period
   - Detailed hours grid
   - Approval status

### 3.4 Travel Requests

#### Submit a Travel Request

1. **Navigate to Travel Requests**
   - Click "My Requests" → "Travel Request" in the sidebar

2. **Fill Out Travel Request Form**
   - **Destination**: Enter travel destination
   - **Purpose**: Select purpose (Business Trip, Training, Conference, etc.)
   - **Start Date**: Travel start date
   - **End Date**: Travel end date
   - **Estimated Cost**: Estimated expenses
   - **Notes**: Additional details

3. **Submit Request**
   - Click "Submit Travel Request"
   - Request is routed to supervisor for approval
   - You'll receive notifications on approval status

#### Track Travel Request Status

- View all your travel requests with status
- Approved requests can be tracked through completion
- Upload receipts and documents after travel

### 3.5 Grievance Reporting

#### File a Grievance

1. **Navigate to Grievance**
   - Click "My Requests" → "Grievance" in the sidebar

2. **Submit Grievance Form**
   - **Category**: Select grievance type
   - **Subject**: Brief description
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation
   - **Attachments**: Upload supporting documents (if any)

3. **Submit**
   - Click "Submit Grievance"
   - Grievance is sent to HR Manager and CEO for review
   - Your grievance is confidential and handled with discretion

#### Track Grievance Status

- View your submitted grievances
- Status updates: Submitted → Under Review → Resolved
- Receive notifications when status changes

### 3.6 Safeguarding Reports

#### Submit a Safeguarding Case

1. **Navigate to Safeguarding**
   - Click "My Requests" → "Safeguarding" in the sidebar

2. **Submit Report Form**
   - **Category**: Type of safeguarding issue
   - **Description**: Detailed report
   - **Urgency**: Select urgency level
   - **Anonymity**: Choose if you want to remain anonymous

3. **Submit**
   - Click "Submit Report"
   - Case is immediately sent to HR Manager and CEO
   - Immediate action is taken on urgent cases

**Important**: Safeguarding reports are treated with the highest priority and confidentiality.

### 3.7 Performance Self-Assessment

#### Complete Self-Assessment

1. **Navigate to Performance**
   - Click "My Requests" → "Performance / Self Assessment"

2. **View Active Review Cycles**
   - See any ongoing performance review cycles
   - Click on a review cycle to participate

3. **Complete Self-Assessment**
   - Fill out the self-assessment form
   - Rate yourself on various competencies
   - Provide examples and evidence
   - Submit for supervisor review

4. **Review Process Flow**
   - Self-Assessment → Line Manager Review → Peer Review → Supervisee Review (if applicable)
   - Receive notifications at each stage
   - View final performance appraisal once complete

### 3.8 View Organization Chart

1. Click "My Workspace" → "Organization Chart"
2. View the complete organizational structure
3. See reporting relationships
4. Search for employees
5. Click on any employee to view their profile (if accessible)

### 3.9 Pending Approvals

If you have approval authority (as a supervisor or manager):

1. Click "My Workspace" → "Pending Approvals"
2. View all requests requiring your approval:
   - Leave requests
   - Timesheets
   - Travel requests
   - Performance reviews
   - Expense reports

3. **Approve or Reject**
   - Click on a request to view details
   - Add comments (optional)
   - Click "Approve" or "Reject"
   - Employee will be notified of the decision

## 4. Supervisor Functions

### 4.1 Approving Leave Requests

1. **Access Pending Approvals**
   - Click "Pending Approvals" in the sidebar
   - Filter by type if needed

2. **Review Leave Request**
   - Click on a leave request to view details
   - Check:
     - Employee's leave balance
     - Dates requested
     - Reason and notes
     - Team coverage needs

3. **Make Decision**
   - Click "Approve" or "Reject"
   - Add comments explaining your decision
   - Submit

4. **Workflow**
   - After supervisor approval, request may go to HR Manager
   - Final approval triggers leave balance deduction
   - Employee is notified automatically

### 4.2 Approving Timesheets

1. **Review Timesheets**
   - Navigate to "Pending Approvals"
   - Select a timesheet to review

2. **Verify Hours**
   - Review hours per project per day
   - Check for accuracy and completeness
   - Ensure total hours are reasonable

3. **Approve or Reject**
   - Click "Approve" if hours are correct
   - Click "Reject" with comments if issues found
   - Employee can resubmit after corrections

### 4.3 Performance Reviews

#### Conduct Line Manager Review

1. **Access Performance Reviews**
   - Navigate to "Performance" or "Pending Approvals"
   - View employees' self-assessments

2. **Review Employee Self-Assessment**
   - Read the employee's self-assessment
   - Review provided examples and evidence

3. **Complete Your Review**
   - Rate employee on competencies
   - Provide feedback and comments
   - Set goals for next period
   - Submit review

4. **Review Chain**
   - After your review, it goes to peer reviewers
   - Supervisees may also provide feedback (if applicable)
   - Final appraisal is compiled and shared with employee

### 4.4 Approval Delegation

If you'll be unavailable, you can delegate your approval authority:

1. **Navigate to Settings** → **Approval Delegation**
2. **Create Delegation**
   - Select delegate (must be your direct report or manager)
   - Set start and end dates
   - Select types of requests to delegate
3. **Activate/Deactivate**
   - Delegation is active during specified dates
   - You can deactivate early if needed

## 6. Finance & Payroll

*Note: Finance functions are accessible to Finance Managers, HR Managers, and Administrators.*

### 6.1 Payroll Management

#### Create Payroll

1. **Navigate to Payroll**
   - Click "HR Management" → "Payroll"

2. **Create New Payroll**
   - Click "Create Payroll"
   - Select:
     - **Pay Period**: Month and year
     - **Payment Date**: When employees will be paid
   - Click "Create"

3. **Add Payroll Entries**
   - System may auto-populate based on timesheets and contracts
   - Manually add or adjust entries as needed:
     - Select employee
     - Enter base salary
     - Add allowances (transport, housing, etc.)
     - Deduct taxes and other deductions
     - Calculate net pay

4. **Review Payroll**
   - Review all entries
   - Check calculations
   - Verify totals

#### Payroll Approval Workflow

1. **Submit to Finance**
   - After creating payroll, click "Submit to Finance"
   - Payroll status: DRAFT → PENDING FINANCE APPROVAL

2. **Finance Manager Review**
   - Finance Manager reviews payroll
   - Can approve or request changes
   - Status: PENDING FINANCE APPROVAL → PENDING CEO APPROVAL

3. **CEO Approval**
   - CEO reviews final payroll
   - Approves or rejects
   - Status: PENDING CEO APPROVAL → APPROVED

4. **Processing**
   - After CEO approval, payroll is marked as "PROCESSED"
   - Payslips are generated
   - Employees can view and download payslips

#### View Payslips

1. Navigate to "My Payslips" (for employees)
2. Or view all payslips in Payroll module (for HR/Finance)
3. Click on payslip to view details
4. Download as PDF

### 6.2 Expense Management

#### Submit Expense Report

1. **Navigate to Expenses**
   - Click "HR Management" → "Expenses" (for employees, this may be under "My Requests")

2. **Create Expense Report**
   - Click "New Expense Report"
   - Fill in:
     - **Report Name**: e.g., "January Business Travel"
     - **Period**: Expense period
     - **Description**: Purpose of expenses

3. **Add Expense Items**
   - Click "Add Expense"
   - Enter:
     - **Date**: Expense date
     - **Category**: Travel, Meals, Supplies, etc.
     - **Amount**: Expense amount
     - **Currency**: Currency used
     - **Description**: What was purchased
     - **Receipt**: Upload receipt image
   - Add multiple items

4. **Submit for Approval**
   - Review total amount
   - Click "Submit for Approval"
   - Report goes to supervisor/manager for approval

#### Approve Expense Reports

1. Navigate to "Pending Approvals"
2. Review expense report
3. Verify receipts
4. Approve or reject
5. Approved reports may go to Finance for payment processing

## 9. Troubleshooting

### 9.1 Common Issues

#### Cannot Login

**Problem**: "Invalid credentials" error

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you're using the correct email address
2. Check if Caps Lock is on
3. Click "Forgot Password" to reset password
4. Contact HR if account is locked

#### Email Not Verified

**Problem**: "Please verify your email" message

**Solutions**:
1. Check your email inbox (and spam folder) for verification email
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. Use "Resend Verification Email" on login page
4. Contact HR if email is incorrect

#### Cannot Submit Timesheet

**Problem**: Timesheet submission fails

**Solutions**:
1. Ensure all required fields are filled
2. Check that hours don't exceed 24 per day
3. Verify dates are within valid range
4. Clear browser cache and try again
5. Contact IT support if problem persists

#### Cannot See Pending Approvals

**Problem**: No approvals showing

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you have supervisor/manager role
2. Check if you're assigned as manager to employees
3. Ensure requests are actually pending
4. Check filters in approval list

#### PDF Download Not Working

**Problem**: PDF download fails or doesn't open

**Solutions**:
1. Check browser pop-up blocker settings
2. Ensure PDF viewer is installed
3. Try different browser
4. Check file size (very large files may take time)

### 10.4 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I edit my submitted leave request?**
A: No, once submitted, you cannot edit. You can cancel it and submit a new one if approval hasn't been given yet.

**Q: How far in advance should I submit leave requests?**
A: Check your leave policy for minimum notice requirements. Generally, submit at least 2 weeks in advance for annual leave.

**Q: What if I forget to submit my timesheet?**
A: Contact your supervisor or HR. Late submissions may require approval or adjustment.

**Q: Can I view my payslip before payday?**
A: Payslips are typically available after payroll is processed and approved. Check with HR for exact availability.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Navigate to your profile settings, click "Change Password", enter current and new password, and save.

**Q: What if my supervisor is not available to approve my request?**
A: If your supervisor has set up approval delegation, their delegate will receive the request. Otherwise, contact HR.

**Q: How long are documents stored in the system?**
A: Documents are stored according to your organization's retention policy. Contact HR for specific retention periods.

**Q: Can I access the system from mobile devices?**
A: Yes, the system is responsive and can be accessed from smartphones and tablets through a web browser.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are supported. Internet Explorer is not supported.

**Q: How do I report a bug or system issue?**
A: Contact IT support with details of the issue, including screenshots and steps to reproduce.
